Strays of Vizag Foundation - Animal Welfare

Strays of Vizag Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to the welfare of stray animals in the Vizag area. Our mission is to provide shelter, medical care, and rehabilitation for stray dogs and cats while promoting community awareness about animal rights and responsible pet ownership.

Our main initiatives include:

  • Rescue and rehabilitation of injured and abandoned animals
  • Spay and neuter programs to control stray populations
  • Community outreach and education campaigns
  • Collaboration with local authorities and volunteers
  • Fundraising events to support shelter maintenance and medical care
