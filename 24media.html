24 Media - Parent Company

24 Media is the parent company overseeing a diverse portfolio of media ventures including content creation, digital marketing, brand management, and multimedia production. We focus on delivering innovative solutions that connect brands with their audiences through storytelling and creative strategies.

Our key services include:

  • Digital marketing campaigns tailored for various platforms
  • Content creation across video, photo, and written formats
  • Brand strategy and management consulting
  • Collaborations with influencers and creators
  • Event management and promotions

History

24 Media was founded in 2020 with nothing but a smartphone, a dream, and zero funding. What started as an experimental YouTube setup in a bedroom quickly evolved into a full-fledged media empire. Within just a year, 24 Media had already produced viral content, launched successful campaigns, and built a foundation that many believed would take a decade to achieve.

And here's the unbelievable part: the founder was only in his early teens when he pulled it all off. Most people didn’t believe it — until they saw the numbers, the reach, and the hustle.
